About Us

Hello and Welcome to Hobbypep.com, Hobbypep is an education provider which delivers hands on training, experience, practical workshops towards Aeromodelling; it helps and encourages the student to learn the technology behind making of R/C planes, UAV’s ,R/C cars in an interesting way.

Note:- The workshops conducted by hobbypep are totally practical where in the students learn to design, build, fly and repair various types of model airplanes, multirotor etc. Hobbypep aeromodelling training institute is contributing significantly in promoting aeromodelling in India
